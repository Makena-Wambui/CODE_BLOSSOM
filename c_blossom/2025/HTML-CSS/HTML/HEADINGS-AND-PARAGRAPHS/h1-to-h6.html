The Lion And The Pig

By: Alicia Makena

Chapter 1: The Lion's Den

Once upon a time, in the heart of the jungle, there lived a lion named Simba. He was the king of the jungle and was feared by all the animals. Simba was a proud lion and he loved to show off his strength to the other animals. He would often roar loudly to show how powerful he was.

Chapter 2: The Pig's Pen

In the same jungle, there lived a pig named Porky. Porky was a friendly pig who loved to play with the other animals. He was not as strong as the lion, but he was loved by all the animals in the jungle.

Porky's Dream

Porky had a dream of becoming friends with the lion. He wanted to show Simba that he was not afraid of him and that they could be friends. Porky knew that the lion was powerful, but he believed that he could win him over with his kindness.
