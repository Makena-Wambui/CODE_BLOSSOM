1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees Celsius
  2. Grease a baking tray
  3. Combine the flour, sugar, and baking powder in a bowl
  4. Add the eggs, milk, and butter to the dry ingredients
  5. Mix until well combined
  6. Pour the mixture into the baking tray
  7. Bake for 30 minutes
  8. Remove from the oven and let it cool
  9. Serve and enjoy!
