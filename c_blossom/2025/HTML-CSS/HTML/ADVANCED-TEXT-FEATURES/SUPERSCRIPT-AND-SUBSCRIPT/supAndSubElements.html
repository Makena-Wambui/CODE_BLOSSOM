You may need to mark up items like dates, mathematical equations and chemical formulae so they have the correct meaning. You can do this using <sup> and <sub> elements.

I celebrate my birthday on 10th February of every year

Elements and their chemical formulae:

  • Oxygen - O2
  • Carbon Dioxide - CO2
  • Sulphuric Acid - H2SO4

If x2 is equal to 25 then x must be 5 or -5.
