You can create links or buttons that when clicked on,
will open a new outgoing email message;
by using <a> and mailto: URL scheme

In its most basic and commonly used form, a mailto: link indicates the email address of the intended recipient.

Send email to Alicia

You can also leave out the email address.
You can omit it so your href is "mailto:"
The user's email client will open a new outgoing email window with no destination email address.
Often useful as Share links that users can click on to send an email to a friend.

Share

You can specify other details like cc, bcc, subject and body.
